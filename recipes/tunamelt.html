Tuna Melt

Description

A Tuna Melt is an open faced, hot tuna sandwich with cheese melted on. Sometimes it also includes other toppings. This image was sourced from here.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna, drained
  • 1 quarter cup mayo
  • 2 slices of bread, preferably whole wheat
  • 2 tablespoons of capers (optional)
  • 1 cup of cheese, preferably grated cheddar

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the tuna and capers and mayo
  2. Divide tuna mix onto bread slices
  3. Cover evenly with cheese
  4. Place into broiler on high until sufficiently melty
