Nutella Sandwich

Description

This recipie is for when you want chocolate cake, but you don't have the right ingredients, and you're willing to lower your standards by a couple notches.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of soft whole wheat bread
  • As much nutella as you can envision putting between the slices of bread

Steps

  1. Lay out the bread slices
  2. Spread the nutella on each slice
  3. Combine the slices together, nutella sides inward
  4. Consume
