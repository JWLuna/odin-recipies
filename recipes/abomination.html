The Culinary Abomination

Description

This recipie is a culinary abomination, and probably a sin against the gods of food. It's also really good if you have a hangover.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna, drained
  • 1 Amy's 3 Cheese Kale Bake bowl
  • 1-3 squirts of siracha

Steps

  1. Microwave the Kale Bake according to the box instructions
  2. Mix in the tuna
  3. Top with siracha
  4. Enjoy!
